Bangor Garden Club

Why Join Us

  • Grow your knowledge through our educational programs
  • Share creative solutions on designing gardens and landscapes
  • Ask for advice about plants in your garden
  • Promote environmental causes
  • Save money at nearby garden centres with our club membership
  • Learn about flower arranging and horticulture
  • Participate in our projects
  • Build lasting friendships with people who share your interests

What we do at our meetings

  • Seed collecting - make your own seed packet and seed box
  • Sow hardy annuals, cornflowers, marigolds and sweet peas
  • Herbs division and semi-ripe cuttings
  • Plant bulbs in pots
  • Bug hotels or bird feeders
  • Make a plastic bottle composter
  • Sow or plant onions and broad beans in pots
  • Take hard wood cuttings
  • Make willow stars and twiggy reindeers
  • Herbs - learn to prick out parsley seedlings and other herbs, root cuttings of mint, and make a herb collection
