Bangor Garden Club

A club for beginner and experienced gardeners

Who we are

Bangor garden Club is an organisation that aims to promote the love of gardening, floral design, civic and environmental responsibility

Why Join

  • Grow your knowledge through our educational programs
  • Share creative solutions on designing gardens and landscapes
  • Ask for advice about plants in your garden
  • Promote environmental causes
  • Save money at nearby garden centres with our club membership
  • Learn about flower arranging and horticulture
  • Participate in our projects
  • Build lasting friendships with people who share your interests
